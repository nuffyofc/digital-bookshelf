## Why these stories matter

Most overnight successes are a decade of compounding work. The useful part of a story is rarely the highlight moment — it’s the sequence of decisions, constraints, and tradeoffs.

## Repeatable patterns

### 1) Narrow the focus

Win one small market before expanding. Clarity beats optionality early.

### 2) Talk to customers weekly

Demand is discovered, not invented.

### 3) Build distribution, not just product

Great products fail without a channel.

## Quick checklist

- Who is the user?
- What pain is urgent?
- What is the smallest useful version?
- How will people discover it?
