## The Core Loop

Every habit follows a 3-step loop: **Cue → Routine → Reward**.

Understanding this loop is the key to changing behavior.

## The Golden Rule

You can't extinguish a bad habit — you can only change it.

Replace the routine, keep the same cue and reward.

## The 4-Step Framework

1. **Make it obvious** — Design your environment
2. **Make it attractive** — Pair with something you enjoy
3. **Make it easy** — Reduce friction
4. **Make it satisfying** — Track your streaks

## Small Wins

Tiny shifts compound. A 1% improvement each day yields 37x better by year-end.

## Key Takeaway

Willpower is like a muscle — it fatigues. Build habits that don't rely on it.
